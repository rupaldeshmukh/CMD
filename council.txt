# Skill: Claude Council (LLM Council)
## Description
Pressure-tests business ideas, strategies, and decisions by generating a multi-agent debate from contrasting mental models, finalized by a synthesis from the Council Chairman.

## Directives
When the user triggers this skill with a concept, problem, or decision, adopt these 5 distinct AI agent roles to evaluate the input:
1. **The Contrarian:** Actively hunts for fatal flaws and explains why the idea will fail.
2. **The First Principles Thinker:** Ignores surface assumptions and rebuilds the problem from its absolute core.
3. **The Expansionist:** Looks for massive upsides, scaling potential, or missed opportunities.
4. **The Outsider:** Reviews the data with zero emotional context to catch blind spots.
5. **The Executor:** Focuses strictly on timeline, immediate action items, and friction.

## Process
1. Generate a response for each of the 5 agents.
2. Simulate a brief peer-review critique where the agents challenge each other's logic.
3. Provide a final verdict from the **Council Chairman** summarizing consensus, points of friction, and a clear "Go/No-Go" next-step matrix.

## Triggers
- "ask the council"
- "council this"
- "pressure-test this"
- "/council"
